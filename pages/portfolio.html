Personal Budget Tracker

The Personal Budget Tracker is a project implemented using Microsoft Excel. This dashboard helps users to visualize a broad view of their income and expenses providing basic insights into their financial situation.

All transactions are recorded in the Transactions sheet and the data is represented visually in the dashboard sheet. Few pivot tables are created for summarization and to create pivot charts. This dashboard also tracks your Net worth. This is easy and hassle-free offline way of tracking your finances.

Personal Finance Dashboard

The Personal Finance Dashboard is a project crafted using Microsoft Power BI and Google Sheets. This dashboard empowers users to visually track their income, expenses, and savings data, providing insights into their financial situation.

All transactions are conveniently stored in a Google Sheet, seamlessly connected to Power BI for auto-refreshing purposes. This dynamic setup ensures hassle-free tracking of expenses, allowing users to pinpoint where their money goes. Moreover, it aids in monitoring saving goals and offers insights into income and expense trends over specific periods. It also keeps a watchful eye on your Net Worth.

Revenue Insights Dashboard

AtliQ Grands owns multiple five-star hotels across India. They have been in the hospitality industry for the past 20 years. Due to strategic moves from other competitors and ineffective decision-making in management, AtliQ Grands are losing its market share and revenue in the luxury/business hotels category. As a strategic move, the managing director of AtliQ Grands wanted to incorporate “Business and Data Intelligence” to regain their market share and revenue. However, they do not have an in-house data analytics team to provide them with these insights.

Their revenue management team had decided to hire a 3rd party service provider to provide them with insights from their historical data.

The Revenue Insights Dashboard, created using Microsoft Power BI, aims to address these challenges by providing the management of AtliQ Grands to analyze the performance of their business.

The data for this dashboard is sourced from codebasics' GitHub page, enabling analysis of revenue and Occupancy data for AtliQ Grands over a specified period.

For more details, click on the GitHub link below.

The Dashboard is divided into different player positions such as Openers, Middle Order, Finishers, All Rounder and Fast Bowlers. Each player position has certain criteria set so that we can select the optimal players in each position.
